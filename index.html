Website Tools Jpeg Compress
Use this to compress your images to a very small size, I used this to save space on things like the main BG images and other stuff. Sometimes cuts the size in less than half of what it was before.

Flipnote Player
Play/Export flipnotes from your DSi/3DS SD Card in your browser.

Base64 Guru
Convert media/other stuff into Base64 text. You can use this as a way to store audio/video on your site via legit just text. This would be nothing more than a useful concept if Neocities didn't restrict direct MP3/MP4 videos so its amazing for that. As a result, if you want audio on your site without paying for the Neocities Supporter stuff, this might be useful to you. 50MB is the max from what I can tell. Still thats very useful for audio and smaller video files.
