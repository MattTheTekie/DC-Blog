Red Crystal RPG

In the beginning, there were 3 crystals. Crystal of Fire, Crystal of Water, and Crystal of Grass. However in 1372, they merged into 1 due to the War of Elements. Ever since 1372, they have been the "red crystal". All societies have agreed to protect this crystal and have for 100s of years until the year 2000 where the group Great Bitches Reborn (GBR) decided to attempt to destroy it. They failed to destroy it but they are attempting to figure it out as we speak. GBR members are all unknown to the rest of society.

The Anti-Great Bitches Reborn (AGBR) group have been attempting to recover the crystal for years with no luck. The red crystal was made immortal when they merged otherwise the crystal would have probably been destroyed. There is only one thing that can destroy it but the element is unknown. This element is known to be able to destroy anything in the word.
